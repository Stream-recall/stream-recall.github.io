Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Data Collection

Stream Recall does not collect, transmit, or store any personal information on external servers. All watchlist data — titles, categories, watched status, watch progress, and preferences — is stored locally on your device only and never leaves your device.

Third-Party Services

When you search for a title or view streaming availability, your search query and the title you are looking up are sent to our database hosted on Supabase. No personal information is included in these requests. Poster images are loaded from the TMDB image CDN. Neither service receives any information that could identify you.

Please review the Supabase Privacy Policy and the TMDB Privacy Policy for details on their data practices.

Data Attribution

Movie and TV show data, including titles, descriptions, poster images, and streaming availability, is sourced from The Movie Database (TMDB). This product uses the TMDB API but is not endorsed or certified by TMDB.

Advertising

This app uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data to serve personalized ads, subject to your consent. On iOS, you will be asked for permission via the App Tracking Transparency prompt. On Android and in regions where consent is required, a consent form will be presented before ads are shown. You may withdraw consent at any time by adjusting your device privacy settings.

Please review Google's Privacy Policy for details on how AdMob handles data.

Analytics and Tracking

Other than advertising (described above), this app does not use any analytics services or crash reporting tools.

Children's Privacy

This app is rated 4+ and does not knowingly collect any information from users of any age.

Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy changes, the updated version will be published at this URL with an updated "Last updated" date.
